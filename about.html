About Kariuki

I am a graduate student with a Degree in Corporate Communication and Management from Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology.

As a result of working within the Construction industry as an intern at Crown Paints Kenya PLC within the Business Development Department for more than five months, I have gained experience and appropriate skills within the professional sector.

Due to my hard work, I received an extension to continue working until I finished school and graduated in June, 2025.

My interest in coding and development came about as a result of the exposure the school has given me, whereby I learned the value of having IT skills such as coding. This aroused my curiosity, and thus I began my coding journey, where the Power Learning Project made that dream a success.

Currently, I am an avid developer with a passion for creating practical, user-friendly, and visually appealing digital experiences. I am slowly growing my skills and portfolio, gaining aptitude to create using languages such as Python and CSS.

In my free time, I enjoy reading books on technology and business, exploring new programming languages, and contributing to open-source projects.

With skills spanning web development, design, and problem-solving, I enjoy building solutions that combine aesthetics and functionality.
