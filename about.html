About Kariuki

My Journey

I am a graduate student with a Degree in Corporate Communication and Management from Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology.

As a result of working within the Construction industry as an intern at Crown Paints Kenya PLC within the Business Development Department for more than five months, I have gained experience and appropriate skills within the professional sector.

As a result of my hard work, I received an extension to continue working until I finished school and graduated in June, 2025.

My interest in coding and Development came about as a result of the exposure the school has given me, whereby I had the opportunity to learn about the value of having IT skills such as coding. This aroused my curiosity, and thus I began my coding journey, whereby Power Learning Project was able to make that dream a success.

Currently I am an avid developer with a passion for creating practical, user-friendly, and visually appealing digital experiences, slowly growing my skills and portfolio while gaining an aptitude to create using languages such as Python and CSS.

In my free time, I enjoy reading books on technology and business, exploring new programming languages, and contributing to open-source projects.
